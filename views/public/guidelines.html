Community Guidelines

These are a set of rules we believe you should follow when playing Place 2.0. Depending on the severity, violations of these rules may result in a permanent ban.

Rules

No Gore

Do not share or post graphic images for sadistic pleasure, glorification or celebration of violence. Place 2.0 is targeted at many age ranges.

No offensive symbols or depictions

This includes swastika's, and any symbols relating to terrorism.

No targeted harassment

Please respect every player: do not threaten to harm another person or someone related to another person in any capacity, do not make art depecting others in a negative way.

No offensive words, phrases, or sentences

This includes excessive slurs, racism, and sexism.

Do not bot

The aim of Place 2.0 is a social game where everyone can hang out, and create cool art. We have anti-botting measures in place, so do not try and circumvent them.

Do not make alternate accounts

We limit all players to a single account; to ensure fairness for everyone accross the board.

Do not make accounts solely to grief

We welcome griefing to make space for new art, however, if you are not going to contribute anything other than trashing art, then you're not welcome here.

Reporting rule breakers

If you notice any violations of these rules, please follow the steps below, whilst we work on a report button.

  • Join our discord
  • Click #report-abuse
  • List the user(s) breaking the rules, along with screenshots or other proof
